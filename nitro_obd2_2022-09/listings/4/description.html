1. Working principle
The power upgrade box is based on the OBD2 protocol and works by remapping and adjusting the trip computer. After the car travels 200km, the power upgrade box will automatically adjust according to the owner's driving habits to adapt to the car's on-board computer to enhance engine performance. According to the driving habits of the car owner, this product can readjust the trip computer ECU to achieve the purpose of increasing the engine power. It will automatically adjust according to the increase of the mileage of the car, so as to adapt to the changes in the driving habits of the car owner and achieve the optimal effect of increasing the power
2. Product introduction
The power upgrade box is a plug-and-play power upgrade box. When this product is inserted into the OBD interface, it can automatically read the data of the on-board computer ECU. Through the data of these ECUs, the boost pressure, fuel quantity, fuel injection time and pressure can be adjusted to increase the engine performance. This product just unearths the conservative power settings hidden before the car leaves the factory, and only works within the limited power range of the engine. It will not cause any damage and other adverse effects to the engine and trip computer ECU. If you want to restore factory settings, just unplug the device.
