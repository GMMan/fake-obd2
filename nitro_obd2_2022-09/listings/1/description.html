What is OBD2 Chip Tuning Box?

OBD2 is a Chip Tuning Box which plugs into the OBD2 connector of your car to increase the performance of your car. When plugged in to the OBD2 connector, EcoOBD2 receives the information from the car ECU.

OBD2 adjusts the boost pressure, quantity of fuel,injection timing and pressure to increase the performance of your car. OBD2 Chip Tuning Box does not exceed the manufacturer requirements.

OBD2 does not damage the engine or the ECU and will not cause any negative effects to the car factory setting. Engine protection is built in.OBD2 does not change any of the settings permanently.

OBD2 Can be swapped from car to easily, unlike a remap which is fixed.OBD2 works on the basis of changing the signal from the ECU. As a consequence, it changes the whole injection map.

The main difference between OBD2 Chip Tuning Box and Remapping tuning methods is that OBD2 Chip Tuning Box is easily removable, and after it is unplugged the ECU reverts back to the factory settings.

OBD2 is equipped with the engine protection function and 100% safe for your car engine. OBD2 produced on the printed circuit board in SMD technology. OBD2 Chip Tuning Boxes are of extremely high quality.

OBD2 Chip Tuning Box Installation and Calibration:

 

1. Pull the Car Key out from the ignition.

2. Find OBD2 connector(*) in your car and Plug in OBD2 Chip Tuning Box

3. Insert the key into the ignition and twist the key to the first stage. (Do not start the car)

4. Press the reset buttom for about 5 sec. After releasing the buttom, just wait for a while about 30-54 sec. (OBD2 Chip Tuning Box will communicate and establish connection with ECU)

5. Start up the engine.

6. OBD2 Chip Tuning Box will recognize your vehicle and your driving habits, after around 200 km/150 miles driving and thus Eco OBD2 Performance Chip Tuning Box will adjust itself to match your car perfectly for increasing the power.
